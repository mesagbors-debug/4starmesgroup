About 4STARSMES GROUP LLC

Comprehensive Multi-Sector Solutions

4STARSMES GROUP LLC is a diversified professional services firm delivering audit-grade compliance, specialized training, digital engineering, hospitality operations, and real estate asset management across pharmaceutical, biotech, technology, and commercial property portfolios.

Our Mission

To empower global organizations with synchronized, precision-focused solutions that drive regulatory excellence, operational integrity, technological advancement, and sustainable revenue growth across five core business pillars.

What We Do

  • Regulatory Compliance & Life Sciences: FDA/EMA audit readiness, process validation, data integrity, and quality systems for pharmaceutical and biotech manufacturers.
  • GMP Training: Role-based workshops and certification programs covering ALCOA+, Annex 1, aseptic handling, and CAPA investigations.
  • Full-Stack Web Freelancing: Production-ready React/Next.js applications, secure APIs, cloud deployment, and performance optimization.
  • Premium Laundry & Cleaning Services: Five-star Airbnb turnovers, commercial laundry, and specialized disinfection for sensitive environments.
  • Commercial Real Estate & Renting: Distressed asset acquisition, leasing strategies, portfolio optimization, and operational management.

Meet Edwin Mesagbor

Quality Assurance Training Supervisor & Founder

Edwin Mesagbor is a Quality Assurance Training Supervisor with over 17 years of experience in manufacturing, food safety, and quality systems management. He holds a bachelor's degree in microbiology and associate degrees in Science Laboratory Technology and Process Technology (High Honors), giving him a strong foundation in public health safety, product quality, and process optimization.

Edwin specializes in regulatory compliance, product safety, and continuous process improvement, with a strong emphasis on FDA regulations, cGMP standards, and data integrity principles. He is an active member of ISPE and ASQ, reflecting his commitment to maintaining high standards in pharmaceutical manufacturing and quality systems.

He is committed to building long-term partnerships with clients who value regulatory excellence, operational transparency, and continuous improvement.
